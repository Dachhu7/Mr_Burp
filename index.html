About Us

Welcome to Mr. Burp

Mr. Burp began with a memory. Growing up in Dubai, founder Simone Bhogal would run to the local grocery every morning for freshly delivered chicken sandwiches. They were simple, delicious, and always there when hunger struck. For kids, these weren’t snacks — they were wholesome meals that kept them fueled throughout the day.

Bringing that same experience to India, Mr. Burp was born. Especially for the bustling 9–5 professionals in Bangalore who often skip meals because of hectic schedules. With Mr. Burp, no one has to miss a meal.

We prepare every sandwich just hours before it reaches your desk, event, café, or store. Fresh veggies, high-quality protein, and thoughtful recipes — turning a simple sandwich into a meal that’s both comforting and nourishing.
